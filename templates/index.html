How the Model Calculates Your Premium
Input Collection
Pincode, weekly earnings, delivery type, platform, experience, cold chain type, deliveries per day, no-claim history, and seasonal index are collected as the 19-feature input vector.
01
Pincode Risk Lookup
Your 6-digit pincode maps to a risk profile — flood score, heat index, AQI exposure, and cyclone probability — derived from historical IMD weather data across 75+ zones in 14 Indian cities.
02
Random Forest Prediction
A trained Random Forest regression model (R² = 0.9861, MAE = ₹3.82) processes all 19 features simultaneously — including seasonal multipliers and delivery type weights — to predict your base premium.
03
Fairness Cap Applied
The final premium is capped at 15% of weekly earnings — ensuring low-income riders are never priced out of coverage. No-claim history (up to 20% discount) is also applied.
